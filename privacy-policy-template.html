Welcome to ZipMate! This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App"). Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with the terms of this privacy policy, please do not access the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you provide directly to us, including:

  • Account Information: Email address, password (encrypted), full name
  • Profile Information: Photos, bio, gender, interests, location details (pincode, locality, city, state)
  • Profile Prompts: Answers to profile questions
  • Verification Data: Verification photos and status

1.2 Location Information

We collect your location data (pincode, locality, city, state) to:

  • Match you with people in your area
  • Provide location-based filtering features
  • Prevent fake profiles and ensure authenticity

Note: Your exact GPS coordinates are NOT stored. We only store your pincode and general location information (locality, city, state).

1.3 Usage Information

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

  • Device information (device type, operating system)
  • App usage data (features used, interactions)
  • Crash reports and error logs (for app improvement)

1.4 Messages and Communications

We store your chat messages and communications with other users within the App.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Create and manage your account
  • Match you with other users based on location and preferences
  • Enable communication between matched users
  • Verify user profiles and prevent fake accounts
  • Provide customer support
  • Improve and optimize the App
  • Ensure safety and security (detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and illegal activity)
  • Send you important updates about the App

3. How We Share Your Information

3.1 With Other Users

When you use ZipMate, certain information is visible to other users:

  • Your profile photos and bio
  • Your profile prompts and answers
  • Your verification status
  • Your general location (pincode, locality, city) - NOT your exact address

Your email address and exact location are NEVER shared with other users.

3.2 With Service Providers

We use third-party services to operate the App:

  • Supabase: For database, authentication, storage, and real-time features
  • Firebase: For crash reporting and analytics (anonymized data)
  • Google Play Services: For location services

These service providers have access to your information only to perform services on our behalf and are obligated not to disclose or use it for any other purpose.

3.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

4. Data Storage and Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Data encryption in transit and at rest
  • Secure authentication systems
  • Regular security audits
  • Access controls and authentication

However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure. While we strive to use commercially acceptable means to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security.

5. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. If you delete your account, we will delete or anonymize your personal information, except where we are required to retain it by law or for legitimate business purposes.

6. Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Update: Update or correct your profile information at any time
  • Delete: Delete your account and personal information
  • Opt-out: Opt-out of certain data collection (may limit app functionality)
  • Report: Report users who violate our terms of service
  • Block: Block users you don't want to interact with

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information provided below.
